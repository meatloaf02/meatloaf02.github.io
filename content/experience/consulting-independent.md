## Independent Consulting

### Overview

Providing strategic product management and higher education consulting services to institutions and organizations seeking to optimize their technology investments and operational processes.

### Service Areas

**Product Strategy & Management**
- Product roadmap development and prioritization
- Market analysis and competitive positioning
- Product vision and strategy definition
- Go-to-market planning

**Higher Education Technology**
- Student Information Systems optimization
- Financial Aid system implementation and enhancement
- Enrollment management solutions
- Technology vendor selection and evaluation

**Organizational Development**
- Agile transformation and coaching
- Team structure and process optimization
- Change management
- Product team training and development

**Data & Analytics**
- Data-driven decision making frameworks
- Performance metrics and KPI development
- Institutional research and analytics
- Predictive modeling and forecasting

### Consulting Approach

Combining deep product management expertise with higher education domain knowledge to deliver practical, impactful solutions that drive measurable results.

**Methodology:**
1. **Discovery:** Understanding organizational goals, challenges, and context
2. **Analysis:** Assessing current state and identifying opportunities
3. **Strategy:** Developing actionable recommendations and roadmaps
4. **Implementation:** Supporting execution with hands-on guidance
5. **Measurement:** Tracking outcomes and optimizing for continuous improvement

### Engagement Models

- Strategic advisory (ongoing)
- Interim product leadership
- Project-based consulting
- Workshops and training
- Executive coaching
